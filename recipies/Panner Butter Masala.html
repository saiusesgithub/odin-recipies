Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala, also known as Paneer Makhani, is a rich and creamy Indian curry. It features soft paneer (Indian cheese) in a tomato-based sauce, flavored with butter, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices. This dish is known for its smooth texture and a balance of sweet and tangy flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Cashews
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (like garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder)

Steps:

  1. Soak Cashews: Soak cashews in hot water for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Make Tomato Puree: Blend tomatoes into a smooth puree.
  3. Prepare Gravy: Saut√© ginger-garlic paste in butter, then add tomato puree and spices. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add Cashew Paste: Grind the soaked cashews into a paste and add it to the gravy.
  5. Simmer: Add water to adjust the consistency and simmer the gravy.
  6. Add Paneer: Gently add paneer cubes to the gravy.
  7. Finish: Stir in cream and garnish with coriander leaves.
