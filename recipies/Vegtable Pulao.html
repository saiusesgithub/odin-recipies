Vegetable Pulao

Vegetable Pulao is a comforting and fragrant one-pot rice dish made with basmati rice, a mix of colorful vegetables, and aromatic spices. It's a simple yet flavorful dish that's easy to prepare and makes for a satisfying meal. The vegetables and spices are sautéed and then cooked with the rice in broth or water, allowing the flavors to blend beautifully.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice
  • Mixed Vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes)
  • Onions
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies
  • Spices (like cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods)
  • Oil or Ghee
  • Coriander leaves

Steps:

  1. Soak Rice: Wash and soak basmati rice for about 20 minutes.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Heat oil or ghee in a pot and add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon, and cardamom. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Sauté Vegetables: Add sliced onions and green chilies and sauté until golden. Then, add ginger-garlic paste and mixed vegetables. Cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add Rice and Liquid: Add the soaked rice and the required amount of water or vegetable broth. Season with salt.
  5. Cook: Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  6. Garnish: Fluff the rice gently and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
